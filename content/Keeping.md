
Keep Reef Notes
===============

Important Key Points
--------------------

## Corals have three key components:

 * Light,
 * Water flow,
 * Nutrition.

## In particular:

 * Great confidence in lighting is critical.
 * 8-12 hour light.
 * Nutrition - low phosphate and nitrate ensures low algae growth
